About Our OQ-TEAP-LAVASH

OQ-TEAP-LAVASH Fried lavash is a national chain of breaded fried chicken specialties. The company has been successfully operating since 2014 and is gradually expanding its network throughout the Republic. Despite the fact that the company operates in the format of a fast food restaurant, the organization of the kitchen is arranged on the principle of a hot shop, i.e. when preparing dishes, exclusively fresh and high-quality products are used, dishes are prepared under the knife and served hot. The menu offers a wide variety of dishes prepared from chicken meat: burgers, twisters, longers, etc. The signature dish is a chicken in the author's breading. In addition to hot dishes, salads, snacks (snacks), drinks, desserts, as well as a special children's menu, taking into account the requirements for children's nutrition, are also presented.
